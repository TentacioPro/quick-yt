Motion & Feedback

Motion in QuickYT is purposeful. It exists to bridge contexts, provide immediate confirmation of actions, and mask necessary latency during data retrieval. We prioritize high-performance CSS transitions and deterministic skeleton states over indefinite spinners.

hourglass_empty Skeleton Loading States

Used during initial data fetch to establish layout expectations. The shimmer effect moves left-to-right at 1.5s per cycle.
